Instruction Set
===============

The assembler simulator supports the following instructions:

+---------- +-----------+------------+-----------+
| ``ADD``   | ``ADDB``  | ``AND``    | ``ANDB``  |
+-----------+-----------+------------+-----------+
| ``CALL``  | ``CLI``   | ``CMP``    | ``CMPB``  |
+-----------+-----------+------------+-----------+
| ``DEC``   | ``DECB``  | ``DIV``    | ``DIVB``  |
+-----------+-----------+------------+-----------+
| ``HLT``   | ``IN``    | ``INC``    | ``INCB``  |
+-----------+-----------+------------+-----------+
| ``IRET``  | ``JA``    | ``JAE``    | ``JB``    |
+-----------+-----------+------------+-----------+
| ``JBE``   | ``JC``    | ``JE``     | ``JMP``   |
+-----------+-----------+------------+-----------+
| ``JNA``   | ``JNAE``  | ``JNB``    | ``JNBE``  |
+-----------+-----------+------------+-----------+
| ``JNC``   | ``JNE``   | ``JNZ``    | ``JZ``    |
+-----------+-----------+------------+-----------+
| ``MOV``   | ``MOVB``  | ``MUL``    | ``MULB``  |
+-----------+-----------+------------+-----------+
| ``NOT``   | ``OR``    | ``ORB``    | ``OUT``   |
+-----------+-----------+------------+-----------+
| ``POP``   | ``POPB``  | ``PUSH``   | ``PUSHB`` |
+-----------+-----------+------------+-----------+
| ``RET``   | ``SHL``   | ``SHLB``   | ``SHR``   |
+-----------+-----------+------------+-----------+
| ``SHRB``  | ``SRET``  | ``STI``    | ``SUB``   |
+-----------+-----------+------------+-----------+
| ``SUBB``  | ``SVC``   | ``XOR``    | ``XORB``  |
+-----------+-----------+------------+-----------+
